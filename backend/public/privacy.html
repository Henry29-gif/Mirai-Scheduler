NurseScheduler is a workforce-scheduling tool used by nursing-home employers to manage staff shifts, time-off, availability and time-clock records. This policy explains what personal data we process and why.

1. Who controls your data

Your employer (the nursing-home facility that gave you an account) is the controller of your data and decides how it is used. We act as a processor on their behalf. Questions about your data are usually best directed to your employer; you may also contact us at [PRIVACY EMAIL].

We do not collect patient health information. We do not use your data for advertising and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

3. How we share it

Your data is visible to authorized staff at your employer (managers/admins) as needed for scheduling. We share data with service providers that host and operate the app (cloud hosting, database, email delivery) under contract, and where required by law. We never sell your personal data.

4. Security

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Access is role-based and passwords are stored hashed. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

5. Retention

We keep employment and timekeeping records for as long as your employer requires them for payroll and legal/compliance purposes. When you delete your account, your personal identifiers (name, email, phone) are removed and your login is disabled; de-identified work records may be retained for payroll/compliance.

6. Your rights & account deletion

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. You can delete your account directly in the app (My Space → Delete account) or follow the steps at [APP URL]/delete-account. Depending on your region you may have additional rights under laws such as GDPR or CCPA.

7. Children

NurseScheduler is for employed adults and is not directed to children under 16.
